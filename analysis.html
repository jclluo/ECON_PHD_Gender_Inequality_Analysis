Key Insights

Placement by Type:

  • 60.48% of candidates were placed in academic positions.
  • 13.73% went to industry roles.
  • 11.08% of candidates became postdocs.

Tabular Representation of Gender Composition

Gender Overall (%) Academic (%) Postdoc (%)
Male 70.65 69.60 68.57
Female 29.35 30.40 31.43

The table presents the gender composition across three categories: overall, academic positions, and postdoc positions. Males comprise ~70% overall, with slightly lower percentages in academic/postdoc roles. Females represent ~29.3% overall, with a modest increase in academic/postdoc (30.4%, 31.4%).
